The Trusted AI Manifesto

13 Commitments. No Exceptions.

AI is an immensely powerful tool. People own accountability, not systems — and people are right to question what they cannot inspect. PRIME's central argument: trust is the gap, and trust must be earned through evidence, not asserted through marketing.

There are no compromises in this pursuit of trust. You have our word and we will show you the evidence.

Trust Architecture

How the Manifesto Becomes Operational.

Trust is earned across the full lifecycle.

Every model, workflow, and integration must be secured, explained, governed, and continuously improved. Deployment is not the end — it is the beginning of the accountability obligation.

Secure

Protect data, models, infrastructure, and workflows at every layer. Security is the bedrock of trust, not an afterthought.

Explain

Reveal assumptions, limitations, and reasoning. People cannot trust what they cannot understand — no black boxes.

Govern

Keep humans responsible for decisions, outcomes, escalation, and oversight. AI does not get the final say.

Improve

Continuously monitor, refine, and harden systems. Trusted AI is never set and forget — the obligation runs the full lifecycle.

Safety Over Speed

Every model, workflow, and integration must be evaluated and validated. PRIME refuses to trade caution for convenience — a standard non-negotiable in federal and DoW environments.

Fairness & Resilience

Bias must be actively identified, measured, and mitigated. Systems must withstand drift, failure, attack, and uncertainty — built to endure, adapt, and recover.

Mission Before Machine

AI is a tool. The mission — protecting people, improving outcomes, strengthening institutions — is the point. We never deploy because we can; only because it helps.

Audit-Ready Architecture

This structure supports future additions — audit criteria, ATO evidence artifacts, certifications, and case studies — without redesigning from scratch.

There are no compromises in this trust pursuit.
